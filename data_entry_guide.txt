PANDUAN DATA ENTRY MYOB MULIA MODISTE
=======================================

1. CASH INFLOW (BUKTI KAS MASUK)
==================================

Langkah-langkah:
1. Buka menu "Banking" > "Receive Money"
2. Deposit To: Pilih "Kas" (1-101)
3. Card: Pilih customer (Pelanggan/LIO TAILOR)
4. Date: Masukkan tanggal transaksi
5. Reference: Masukkan BKM No. (KM-01, KM-02, dll)
6. Amount: Masukkan jumlah diterima
7. Memo: Masukkan keterangan transaksi
8. Account: Pilih revenue account (4-101/4-102)
9. Klik "Record"

Contoh Entry:
- Date: 09/03/2019
- Reference: KM-03
- Card: Pelanggan
- Amount: 850,000
- Memo: Upah jahit dari langganan
- Account: 4-101 Pendapatan Jahit

2. CASH OUTFLOW (BUKTI KAS KELUAR)
====================================

Langkah-langkah:
1. Buka menu "Banking" > "Spend Money"
2. Pay From: Pilih "Kas" (1-101)
3. Card: Pilih supplier
4. Date: Masukkan tanggal transaksi
5. Reference: Masukkan BKK No. (KK-01, KK-02, dll)
6. Amount: Masukkan jumlah dibayar
7. Memo: Masukkan keterangan transaksi
8. Account: Pilih expense account sesuai jenis
9. Klik "Record"

Contoh Entry:
- Date: 01/03/2019
- Reference: KK-01
- Card: Surianto
- Amount: 900,000
- Memo: Sewa kios untuk 3 bulan (Maret - Mei 2019)
- Account: 5-102 Beban Sewa Kios

3. SALES INVOICE (FAKTUR PENJUALAN)
====================================

Langkah-langkah:
1. Buka menu "Sales" > "Enter Sales"
2. Card: Pilih customer (LIO TAILOR)
3. Date: Masukkan tanggal invoice
4. Invoice No.: Masukkan No. Faktur (F0301, F0302)
5. Terms: Pilih payment terms (n/15)
6. Item: Pilih item/service
7. Quantity: Masukkan jumlah
8. Price: Masukkan harga per unit
9. Account: Pilih revenue account (4-101/4-102)
10. Klik "Record"

Contoh Entry:
- Date: 15/03/2019
- Invoice No.: F0301
- Card: LIO TAILOR
- Terms: n/15
- Item: OBR001 Jasa Obras
- Quantity: 1
- Price: 200,000
- Account: 4-102 Pendapatan Obras

4. PURCHASE BILL (FAKTUR PEMBELIAN)
====================================

Langkah-langkah:
1. Buka menu "Purchases" > "Enter Purchases"
2. Card: Pilih supplier
3. Date: Masukkan tanggal bill
4. Purchase No.: Masukkan No. Faktur supplier
5. Terms: Pilih payment terms
6. Item: Pilih item
7. Quantity: Masukkan jumlah
8. Price: Masukkan harga per unit
9. Account: Pilih expense/asset account
10. Klik "Record"

Contoh Entry:
- Date: 07/03/2019
- Purchase No.: TM01
- Card: Toko MAJU
- Terms: n/15
- Item: PLG001 Perlengkapan Jahit
- Quantity: 1
- Price: 60,000
- Account: 5-103 Beban Perlengkapan Jahit

5. JOURNAL ENTRY (BUKTI MEMORIAL)
==================================

Langkah-langkah:
1. Buka menu "Accounts" > "Record Journal Entry"
2. Date: Masukkan tanggal adjustment
3. Reference: Masukkan BM No. (M-0301, M-0302, dll)
4. Memo: Masukkan keterangan adjustment
5. Debit Account: Pilih account yang didebit
6. Credit Account: Pilih account yang dikredit
7. Amount: Masukkan jumlah adjustment
8. Klik "Record"

Contoh Entry:
- Date: 31/03/2019
- Reference: M-0301
- Memo: Jasa jahit yang telah selesai tetapi belum diterima
- Debit Account: 1-102 Piutang Usaha
- Credit Account: 4-101 Pendapatan Jahit
- Amount: 150,000

6. PAYMENT RECORDING
====================

A. Pay Bills (Pembayaran Hutang):
1. Buka menu "Purchases" > "Pay Bills"
2. Pilih supplier dan bill yang akan dibayar
3. Masukkan payment details
4. Klik "Record"

B. Receive Money (Penerimaan Piutang):
1. Buka menu "Sales" > "Receive Money"
2. Pilih customer dan invoice yang akan dibayar
3. Masukkan payment details
4. Klik "Record"

7. ADJUSTING ENTRIES
=====================

A. Accrued Expenses:
- Debit: Expense Account
- Credit: Liability Account

B. Prepaid Expenses:
- Debit: Asset Account
- Credit: Expense Account

C. Depreciation:
- Debit: Depreciation Expense
- Credit: Accumulated Depreciation

D. Accrued Revenue:
- Debit: Asset Account
- Credit: Revenue Account

8. CLOSING ENTRIES
==================

A. Close Revenue Accounts:
- Debit: Revenue Account
- Credit: Income Summary

B. Close Expense Accounts:
- Debit: Income Summary
- Credit: Expense Account

C. Close Income Summary:
- Debit/Credit: Income Summary
- Credit/Debit: Owner's Equity

9. DATA VALIDATION
==================

Sebelum posting transaksi, pastikan:
1. Date format benar (DD/MM/YYYY)
2. Amount format benar (tanpa koma ribuan)
3. Account numbers valid
4. Card names valid
5. Reference numbers unique
6. Debit = Credit (untuk journal entries)
